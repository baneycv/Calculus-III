    Solution:

    By using a double integral and polar coordinates, find the volume of the region bounded above by the plane z=4+x+2⁢y, on the sides by the cylinder x2+y2=1, and below by the x⁢y plane. Note: Define V as a double integral for credit.
    Solution:

    To evaluate this, we use polar coordinates:

    x=r⁢cos⁡θ,y=r⁢sin⁡θ,d⁢A=r⁢d⁢r⁢d⁢θ

    Therefore, the volume is:

    V=∫02⁢π∫01(4+r⁢cos⁡θ+2⁢r⁢sin⁡θ)⁢r⁢𝑑r⁢𝑑θ

    We expand the integrand:

    V=∫02⁢π∫01(4⁢r+r2⁢cos⁡θ+2⁢r2⁢sin⁡θ)⁢𝑑r⁢𝑑θ

    Now integrate with respect to r:
